CONSHARETO is a photo-sharing app that will encourage music lovers to share their experiences and passions with each other across social media, as well as explore carefully curated lists of new artists and tours in the area.

This is an individual school project that involved user research, wireframing, visual design, and pitch deck design.

Features

People can choose to like, share, and comment on individual photos, or the folder of a specific user, or that specific event.

Users can see what other venues the band is scheduled to play at, or what venues they had previously visited.

Photos appear on the venue page, or the tour page for the band so people can view and share their experience.

Final Screens

CONSHARETO is all about sharing your concert experience, and so interactions and pages are centered heavily around keeping the feeling of going to a live performance, such as ticket stubs and posters. The visuals of CONSHARETO feature dark backgrounds and pops of colors, similar to the lights displayed at concerts.
