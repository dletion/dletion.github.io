Space Race is an installation designed for ImagineRIT, an innovation and creativity festival. It takes the core experience of Bitness and builds a family-friendly group exercise experience that all ages can enjoy. Compete with and against other participants in a friendly race to outer space!

Bitness is an immersive group exercise experience designed for corporate gyms that will promote a healthy, positive, and energetic workforce while building team moreale within the company

This was a group project at RIT as my senior thesis and includes user research, built proof of concept, and was overseen by Adobe's R&D Department

Features

Bitness users can join or create teams, setup workouts, and improve their interpersonal relationships through achieving workout goals together.

Bitness' personalized dashboard allows you to interact with your workout summaries, team stats, and track progress toward your goals to earn achievements.

Bitness users can share their progress to develop a supportive and healthy in-office community and get motivated by real time data visualizers while you work out
